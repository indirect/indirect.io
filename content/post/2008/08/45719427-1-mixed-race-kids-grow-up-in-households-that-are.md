Mixed-race kids manage to be as bad as whites on the white behaviors and as bad as blacks on the black behaviors. Mixed-race kids act out in almost every way measured in the data set.</p>\n<p>— <a href=\"http://freakonomics.blogs.nytimes.com/2008/08/12/the-plight-of-mixed-race-children/\">The Plight of Mixed-Race Children</a></p>"
